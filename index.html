Dice Duel Rules

Objective

Be the first to mark off all 12 numbers on your list.

Basic Gameplay

Players take turns rolling two six-sided dice. You can mark off:

  • A number matching one die
  • A number matching the sum of both dice

Special Rules

Rolling doubles gives an extra turn. If doubles are rolled again, no additional turn is granted.

Scoring

Winner receives 78 points. Opponent scores the sum of their marked numbers.
